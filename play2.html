LORD AUGUSTUS

You’re getting annoying, dear-boy; you’re getting demmed annoying.

CECIL GRAHAM

[_Puts his hands on his shoulders_.]

Now, Tuppy, you’ve lost your figure and you’ve lost your character. Don’t lose your temper; you have only got one.

LORD AUGUSTUS

My dear boy, if I wasn’t the most good-natured man in London—
